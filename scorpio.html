SCORPIO

Information

Scorpio (♏︎) (Ancient Greek: Σκορπιός, romanized: Skorpiós, Latin for "scorpion") is the eighth astrological sign in the zodiac, originating from the constellation of Scorpius. It spans 210–240° ecliptic longitude. Under the tropical zodiac (most commonly used in Western astrology), the Sun transits this sign on average from October 23 to November 21. Depending on which zodiac system one uses, someone born under the influence of Scorpio may be called a Scorpio or a Scorpionic.

How it formed

Scorpio is a constellation, meaning it is a group of stars that appear to form a pattern when viewed from Earth. The constellation of Scorpio has been known since ancient times and was recognized by many cultures around the world. It was named after the scorpion, a creature known for its sting and deadly nature. The zodiac sign Scorpio is based on the position of the Sun in relation to this constellation during the time of year when Scorpio is visible in the night sky. This is how the astrological sign of Scorpio came to be associated with certain personality traits and characteristics.

Where and when to find Scorpio

Scorpio is not visible in the sky year-round, as it is a seasonal constellation. It is visible from late spring to early summer in the Northern Hemisphere and from late autumn to early winter in the Southern Hemisphere. The best time to see Scorpio is during the months of July and August in the Northern Hemisphere and during the months of December and January in the Southern Hemisphere. It can be seen low on the southern horizon in the evening in these months. However, it is important to note that the visibility of Scorpio also depends on various factors such as weather and light pollution in your area.

Fun fact !

A fun fact about Scorpios is that they are known for their intense and passionate personalities. They are often described as mysterious and are known for their ability to keep their emotions hidden. They are also highly intuitive and have a strong ability to read people and situations. Scorpios are said to be powerful and transformative, often going through significant personal growth and change throughout their lives.
